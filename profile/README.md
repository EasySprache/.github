## EasySprache

EasySprache builds accessibility-focused products that make hard-to-read content easier to understand.

Our goal is simple: help people understand written information without making the experience slow, expensive, or difficult to access. Our products turn dense text into easier language and, where useful, can read it aloud.

We build for people who often face barriers with complex text, including people with:

- reading or cognitive difficulties
- dyslexia
- lower literacy
- non-native language backgrounds
- seniors
- anyone who needs clearer, simpler wording

## Products

### KlarText Web App
KlarText is an EasySprache product that allows users to paste text or upload supported content and receive a simpler version in easy language. It also supports text-to-speech so users can listen instead of read.

KlarText focuses on preserving meaning while improving readability through shorter sentences, simpler vocabulary, and clearer structure.

> **Important:** KlarText creates plain-language / easy-language simplifications. It is **not** certified *Leichte Sprache* and does not guarantee legal, medical, or official accuracy.

### KlarText Extension
The KlarText browser extension is another EasySprache product focused on simplifying website content directly where people are already reading. Instead of copying and pasting into another tool, users can simplify content in place while browsing.

### KlarText Feedback Loop
The KlarText feedback loop supports internal evaluation and continuous improvement across the KlarText product ecosystem. It helps us review outputs, compare quality across runs, and improve prompts, rules, and system behavior over time.

## What KlarText Does

- simplifies dense English and German text
- aims to preserve original meaning
- improves readability with shorter, clearer wording
- supports text-to-speech for easier access
- explores in-browser website simplification through the extension
- supports internal review and quality improvement through the feedback loop

## Why This Exists

A lot of important information is still written in ways that are too hard for many people to use. Bureaucratic, medical, legal, and technical language often create unnecessary barriers.

EasySprache exists to reduce that gap by building products that make information clearer, faster to access, and easier to understand.
